**The Unum Accord** is an agreement amongst every nation within [[Mayl]]. This agreement came about after the [[The Magus War|Magus War]]. The accord banned the practice of all forms of magic, only excluding the race of [[Elves]], who have an innate connection to magic. Another decree of the accord, is banning the worship of all god except [[Heron]] and [[Alanus]]. Furthermore, the blaming of dragons for the actions of the [[Sviddian Mages]] led to the order of their extinction.
