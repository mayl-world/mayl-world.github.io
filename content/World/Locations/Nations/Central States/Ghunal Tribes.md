**Languages:** [[Common]], [[Ghulnic]] ([[Primordial]] Based)
The **Ghunal Tribes** are a group tribes that make up a unified [[Nations|nation]]. The Ghunal Tribes are situated within the [[Ghunal Desert]] which is in the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. Their territory is bordered with [[Laudia]], [[The Krystad Dominion]], and [[Pers]]. The tribes consist of male nomads, who form caravans to trade goods within the desert and with neighbors, and settlements, where women defend and take care of the children and elderly. These settlements also farm along the fertile river, and act as security to protect from tribal raids. 

### Societal Structure
Society is structured around hereditary protection. Each tribe is nomadic, travelling between towns and villages around the [[Ghunal Desert]]. The men of these tribes trade goods with the women in exchange for other goods, or services. Usually goods will be given for temporary companionship. This means that the tribes have children scattered across all settlements. When the sons come of age, they leave their home and travel with their tribe, while daughters stay within the settlement producing food, and other goods for their settlement, and their father's tribe.

### Economy
The Ghunal Tribes facilitate a key economic position between the [[Umbracine Sea]], the [[Adunican Peninsula]], and the rest of the [[Southern Continent]]. They bring in goods from each respective region, and act as a mercantile hub for trade.
