### [[Nations]] of the Central States
##### [[Pers]]
##### [[Ghunal Tribes]]

The **Central States** is situated in the north part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. In the north it has the vast [[Ghunal Desert]], with the [[Pers]] steppes in the south. Between the desert and steppes is a rocky river valley, and a ridge that marks the start of the desert.
