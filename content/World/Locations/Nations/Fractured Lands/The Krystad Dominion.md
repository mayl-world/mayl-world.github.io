**Languages**: [[Common]], [[Hillspeak]] (Giant Based)

**The Krystad Dominion** is [[Nations|nation]] within the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]] on the western part of the [[Muldred Spike Mountains]]. It shares a border with [[Felland]] to the north, and the [[Ghunal Tribes]] to the east. The Dominion is made up of 7 domains each ruled by a noble. Citizenship of Krystad is earned through military service, meaning the citizen class is smaller than other nations. 

### Government
The government is split into two bodies, the [[Krystad Senate|Senate]] as a legislator, and the [[Aivors]] as the executive. The senate is made up of 14 senators, two from each dominion and the [[Dominitor]], with the Dominitor representing the capital city of [[Krystad]]. One of these senators is elected by the citizens of that dominion, and the other is appointed by the duke.

The [[Aivors]] are a military group that is led by the [[Aericus]]. The [[Aivors]] are knights that are highest ranking of the Krystad military. They ride giant birds, which they tame and bond with when they are promoted. The [[Aivors]] enforce laws, and solve disputes between domains. The [[Aericus]] also holds the power to veto any laws passed by the Senate, preventing obscure or unenforceable laws.

### Culture
The culture of Krystad is very militaristic, and centered around cultivating power, with the sole way of becoming a citizen by joining the military. These aspiring citizens must make a pilgrimage on foot through the [[Muldred Spike Mountains]] to reach [[Krystad]]. Upon reaching, they must present a tribute of sufficient quality to gain access to the city, and their citizenship. 

Although the main culture is Krystadian, the [[Hill People]] have a unique culture of their own. They value hard work, and their ethnic communities. Most outsiders are distrusted and met with a cold shoulder, while they treat their own warmly. If one can prove themselves as trustworthy, and similar in values to the Hill People, they will be welcomed as one of them. 

### Religion 
Relgion is homogenized within the Krystad Dominion. Almost all of the population follows the saint [[Darshad Guth Haman]], and have festivals to reflect that, the largest being the celebration of the [[Festival of Night]]. These religious beliefs causes people to create strong parental and familial bonds so their children will be guided to their ancestral home within the afterlife.
