**Languages:** [[Common]], [[Hard Tongue]] ([[Giant]] Based)
**Felland** is a [[Nations|nation]] located within the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It shares a border with [[The Kingdom of Sen]] in the north, and [[The Krystad Dominion]] in the south. It has a rocky terrain, with sheer coastal cliffs.

### Government
The government of Felland is autocratic, with a distinct lord ruling over an island. These lords hold considerable amount of power over their island, where they oversee smaller local governments, law enforcement, and general welfare. The islands are mostly autonomous from each other, with interaction being limited to what the lords wish. 

There is very little central power with the [[Sovereign of Felland|Sovereign]] acting only as a ceremonial figurehead of the government. The Sovereign is the figure to interact with external entities, and conduct diplomacy, but nothing stops the lords from making individual private treaties with other nations. The Sovereign also serves as a unifying figure to prevent the lords from fighting amongst themselves.

There is a [[Council of Lords (Felland)|Council of Lords]], made up of the most influential of lords, handle important issues, help settle disputes between islands, as well as enact policy for the entire nation. 

There are also Elders, who function as an advisory class to their lords, the Sovereign, and the Council of Lords. These Elders are usually older members of society that have experience in public service, and has gained wisdom of the years on governance. 

### Culture
The people of Felland are rugged and resilient, much like the terrain they live in. They value a simple life style, and appreciate the land for what it gives them. Self-Sufficiency is a virtue among the people of Felland, as each person is expected to take care of themselves, and not be a burden on others. There is an idea of mutual assistance, where help is given, often as a response to help, or with the expectation of help in return. 

The people of Felland are also cautious towards outsiders, but will be more friendly if shown generosity. 

### Economy
Felland is known for the mining of precious metals and gems within its cliffs. It has many mines gathering copper, iron, and silver. The presence near the sea also brings a large industry of fishing and other sea resources, such as seaweed, salt, etc.
