**Trembius** is a [[Nations|nation]] and city-state located in the [[Halmnic Peninsula]] within the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. Trembius is known for its strong presence on the waters, and skilled sailors. It adamantine rams traded with [[Olis]] it small navy has been able to rival that of [[The Operus Empire]]. The people of Trembius are curious and philosophical, often wondering about what lies beyond the large ocean to the west.
