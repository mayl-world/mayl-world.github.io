**Languages:** [[Myneth]], [[Common]]
**The Operus Empire** is a [[Nations|nation]] located on the [[Halmnic Peninsula]] in the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is ruled by [[Emperor Ordememnon IV]]. The Operus Empire recently began conquering the city states of the [[Halmnic Peninsula]] utilizing its superior naval power, importing mercenaries from [[The Krystad Dominion]]. Although the empire is campaigning against the nearby city states of [[Attika]], [[Magos]], and [[Trembius]], its people live in peace, with the empire only maintaining a small army.
