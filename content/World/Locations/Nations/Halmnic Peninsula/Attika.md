**Languages:** [[Myneth]], [[Common]]
**Attika** is a [[Nations|nation]] located on the [[Halmnic Peninsula]] in the western part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is a small city state known for their combat prowess. The children are raised to handle spears and other weaponry from when they begin taking their first steps. Attika has successfully defended itself from the aggressive expansion of [[The Operus Empire]]. Attika has a slave class that it obtains through battle, or from [[Mardria]].

### Government
Attika is ruled by two kings, who act as checks on each other. The kings are heads of the religion, military, and judiciary. Usually the responsibilities of the judiciary are relegated to a group of elders. 

### Culture
The culture of Attika is brutal for those who are slaves, with disobedience being met with harsh punishments. The citizens enjoy a life of luxury due to this, and spend the free time practicing for battle.
