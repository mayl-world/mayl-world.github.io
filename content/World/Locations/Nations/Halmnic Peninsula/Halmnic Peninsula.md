The **Halmnic Peninsula** is in the western most part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is home to various city-states, who are all attempting to gain influence over one another. [[The Operus Empire]] has recently began taking over city-states, creating a unified nation. 

The land here is rocky, warm, and dry. With mountains holding much of the central land, people settled along the coast, or along the rivers that spring from the rocky ground.
