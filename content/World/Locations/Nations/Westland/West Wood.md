**Languages**: Elvish, Common

**West Wood** is a nation in the western part of the [[Northern Continent]]. 

### Meta Ramblings
West wood is supposed to be the woods filled with wood elves. They live in the densely packed forests, with some of their home built up in the trees. They are esteemed hunters, and use every part of the animals that they hunt. Because of this, they are known for making some bone armor and arrows. 

The nation is isolated due to the [[Vensh Mountains]] to the east. The thick woods, and lack of roads makes it hard for outsiders and importantly invaders to navigate and settle within the west wood.

The woods are governed with the elder elves forming a council. Their long lives give them the wisdom to lead properly. They act in order to preserve the woods. These elves do not need much sustenance, with minimal food. In fact, if a wood elf did nothing but sit in direct sunlight, they would have enough to maintain their body. 

The west wood contains a sweet and glowing fruit called the [[Sunfruit|sunfruit]] that is sought after outside of the west wood. It can be made into a delectable and sweet wine. There is also the [[Red Root|red root]], which is usually dried and ground to be used as a hallucinatory drug.
