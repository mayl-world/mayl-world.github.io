**The Kingdom of Sen** is a [[Nations|nation]] located in the [[Northern Continent]]. It shares a border with [[Bodenland]] to the west, the [[Golden Throne Dwarves]] in the mountains to the north, and [[Oldraynes]] to the east. The land is broken into three sections, the [[Sennic Vale]] in the east, the [[Sen Lowlands|Lowlands]] in the center, and the [[Sen Highlands|Highlands]] to the west. 

The Vale is compromised of low hills, and scattered forests. The Lowlands are filled with lush plains with ever fertile soil, filled with farms. The Highlands to the east are rocky, and sparsely populated.

### Structure of the Kingdom
The Kingdom is well structured, with a system of hierarchy of titles that govern the responsibility of people. At the heart of this hierarchy is the monarch, the King or Queen of Sen. The monarch holds absolute authority over the kingdom, serving as the maker of laws, commander of the army, and arbiter of disputes. The royal family is supported by a council of advisors, mostly of trusted nobles and scholars, that help the king in making decisions. 

The kingdom is divided into duchies, that are governed by their Duke or Duchess. These high-ranking nobles are responsible for maintaining law and order, managing resources, and representing the king's interests. They oversee the governing of their region and work to implement the will of the monarch.

Within each duchy, there are baronies and counties, each led by a Count or a Baron. These nobles handle day-to-day governance, such as collecting taxes, maintaining roads, and dealing with local bandits, criminals, and other threats to the peace. They also appoint judges to handle any disputes between lower class citizens.

### Culture
The people of Sen have a devout sense of duty, usually to their monarch. The people hold their station in the realm to high esteem, with farmer to noble each holding their position with honor. They view the monarch as a symbol of honor, intellect, and wisdom, and as a result will never slander or speak bad of their king or queen. Many hold the monarch is such high regard, that they believe the monarch is blessed with the [[sainthood]] by [[Heron]]. 

The people of Sen also have a high regard for academia. They enjoy writing books and stories about their rich history and tradition. They usually have a drive to understand the world around them, in order to have a greater sense of wisdom and understanding about the worlds natural order. One of [[Mayl|Mayl's]] most prestigious universities, [[The University of Sen]], was founded by the monarch for the sole purpose of learning more about the natural laws of the world. 

The people of Sen are also very hospitable. Due to the large amount of food, and social systems, people are very friendly and will usually welcome strangers with open arms and a hearty meal. Acts of kindness and charity are seen as contributing to the greater good and assisting the monarch in their struggle to keep peace in their realm.
