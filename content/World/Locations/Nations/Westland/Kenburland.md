**Languages**: [[Common]], [[West Tongue]] ([[Myneth]] Based)

**Kenburland** is a [[Nations|nation]] on the western coast of the [[Northern Continent]]. It shares a border with [[Nestadhof]] to the east, and the [[West Wood]] to the south. 

### Meta Ramblings
There is a central king who all the knights are loyal to. Before the empire, it was just small farming kingdoms. They faced constant raids, and were threatened by the empire. Decided enough is enough, and the man named [[Kenneth Burr]] rallied all of the farming kingdoms, allied with [[Bodenland]] and fought back against the empire. 

They are a kingdom of unity, and resilience, and persistence. They value independence and community. A symbol of this unity is the king, who acts as a central figure, not loyal to any particular lord, but to the nation as a whole.

The [[Council of Lords]], is made up of the lords within Kenburland, and serves as advisor to the king. No decision is made without agreement by the king, and by extension, the agreement by the council.

In recent times, each town has been required to keep its citizenry trained, and to keep an armory to arm them. This is due to frequent raids from [[Nestadhof]], and [[Hjaalland]].

Kenburland currently is attempting to regain lost land, and free its people from Nestadhof, who expanded their territory recently.
