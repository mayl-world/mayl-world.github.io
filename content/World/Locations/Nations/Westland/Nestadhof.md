**Languages**: [[Common]], [[Norshk]] (Giant Based)

**Nestadhof** is a [[Nations|nation]] in the western part of the [[Northern Continent]]. It is bordered by [[Kenburland]] to the west, and [[Bodenland]] to the south. 

### History
Nestadhof was originally a colony of [[Hjaalland]], and was created to be a small base of operations for raids further south. Eventually, when the people discovered the fertile soil, lush woods, and deposits of iron, they settled down more permanently. The colony started off small, but with its neighbors weakened from the constant raiding, it grew quickly. 

In 663 AMW, [[Nestad Grym]] began to conspire against his half-brother, [[King Sveyn Emblesyn| Sveyn Emblesyn]], king of Hjaalland. Later that year, his plot was uncovered and he was exiled for his treasonous acts. Still in pursuit of power, Nestad seized control of the southern colony with an army of his loyalists. He named his new kingdom Nestadhof in honor of his own name, and began expanding his borders ever since.

### Meta Ramblings
Nestadhof is a break off of [[Hjaalland]]. One of the leaders, Nestad Emblesyn, tried to usurp power in Hjaalland.

Many raiders and loyalists would follow him to this land when rumors formed that he was alive. 

The people of Nestadhof are proud warriors. They are vicious an vengeful. If they perceive that you wronged them, they will force you to give them something in return. 

A couple times a year, the people gather and form raiding parties. These raiding parties disperse outside of their own land and participate in a competition on who can gather the most valueables on their raids. Most of these raids are conducted in Kenburland, but many find themselves in [[West Wood]], [[Woods of Coree]], or even in the northern parts of [[The Kingdom of Sen]]. The raiders often avoid [[Bodenland]] out of fear of their warriors. 

Nestadhof also has constant interest in expanding its border and forcing the peoples it conquers into submission, where they are forced to work to create food, weapons, armor, and other goods for Nestadhof.
