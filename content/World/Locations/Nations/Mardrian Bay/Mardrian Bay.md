The **Mardrian Bay** is located in the central part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is a deep bay that gives the [[Southern States]] a connection to the ocean, and by extension the rest of [[Mayl]]. The heavy traffic within the bay has led to large amounts of crime along its shores, mostly at the will of the [[Head of Mardria]].
