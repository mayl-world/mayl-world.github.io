**Firan** is a [[Nations|nation]] located near the [[Mardrian Bay]] in the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It borders the nations of [[Mardria]], [[Tainenterra]], and [[Sholeria]]. Firan is a Shahdom that was founded by the [[Persemon]] remnants of the [[Persemon Empire]]. They are characterized by their beautiful masonry, and stone carvings. Their buildings often showcase intricate mosaics. The shahdom was founded after the passing of [[Shah Dimurlan Bey]]. 

The southern part of Firan sits within the [[Barbartre Woods]], and suffers frequent attacks from [[Sholeria]], [[Tainenterra]], and monsters.

### Government
The central power of Firan is the Shah, who resides in the city of [[Sangbactria]]. The Shah has authority over all subjects of Firan, but separates his powers into various [[Satrapy|Satrapies]]. These Satraps, who govern a Satrapy, are appointed to be extensions of the Shah. 

Each Satrapy has a general who manages the army, and ensures security, and a secretary that keeps record of the office. The general and the secretary report to both the Satrap and the Shah. 

### Conflicts
Firan is currently locked in conflict with [[Sholeria]] as well as [[Tainenterra]]. They are viewed as a threat to their territories, and as outsiders by these nations, and face constant assaults as a result. This is due to their connection to the [[Persemon Empire]], which conquered their lands many years ago.
