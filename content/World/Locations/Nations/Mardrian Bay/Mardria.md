**Languages:** [[Common]]
**Mardria** is a [[Nations|nation]] located on the shores of the [[Mardrian Bay]] in the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is a nation that is surrounded with crime, violence, and slavery. Its entire political structure revolves around local gangs providing protection to those that work for them, and the [[Head of Mardria|Head]] giving large sums of gold to those that do his bidding.

### Government
There is no official governing body within Mardria, but usually rules set by the Head are followed by the gangs out of fear of his ire. 

Within each locality are various gangs, that conduct business, and extort others for money. Almost every person within Mardria is associated with a gang in order to protect themselves from the activities of the other gangs.

### Culture
The people of Mardria are very selfish, often resorting to extortion, robbery, and other violent crimes to increase their prosperity. There are a subset of the population that are attempting to create a cultural change, providing services to those regardless of gang affiliation, but those groups when found are quickly extorted and taken for everything they have.

### Meta Ramblings
Mardria is supposed to be run by a crime lord, who peddles drugs and other illicit goods through the ocean and across Mayl. 

Their proximity to the Sviddian desert means that some settlements are vulnerable to attacks by vicious monsters.

There are many hunters that go into the Sviddian desert in hopes of finding rare and valuable monster body parts, some of which are used to create drugs. These rare and expensive drugs help run Mardria alongside the slave trade it conducts.

Mardria engages in undercover deals with Tainenterra in particular to obtain war prisoners and criminals in exchange for their drugs and other forms of wealth.

The people of Mardria are often distrusting, with various gangs populating the urban areas. Almost everyone in Mardria is a part of a gang, with those unaffiliated being quickly targeted for robbery and other violent crimes. The Head, the leader of Mardria, holds most of the wealth within Mardria, with the various gangs doing their best to do his bidding to grow in prosperity.
