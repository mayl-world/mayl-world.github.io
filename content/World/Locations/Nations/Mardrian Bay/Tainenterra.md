**Tainenterra** is a [[Nations|nation]] located at the mouth of the [[Mardrian Bay]] within the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. Most of the nation is covered by the dangerous [[Barbartre Woods]], with only the shore in the north being outside of the woods. 

### Government
Much like [[Sholeria]], Tainenterra has an absolute king, though he is appointed by a council of sages. There is a small set of humans and elves that live on the northern coast that pay tribute and taxes to the Orc king in exchange for protection and freedom. 

### Meta Ramblings
The main difference between Tainenterra and Sholeria is their proximity to Mardria. Because Tainenterra is closer to Mardria, their king is easily influenced by the Mardrian money that makes its way south. Usually this is in the form of bribes to provide open markets for Mardria, as well as to keep the conflict with Firan ongoing.
