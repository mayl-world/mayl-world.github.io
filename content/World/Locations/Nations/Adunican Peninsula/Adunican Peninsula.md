The **Adunican Peninsula** is located in the eastern part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is the home of the [[The Aurellian Empire]], and [[Hobgoblins]]. The nations of the peninsula are the targets of [[The Unum Tribunal]], and at the heart of [[The Sundering War]].
