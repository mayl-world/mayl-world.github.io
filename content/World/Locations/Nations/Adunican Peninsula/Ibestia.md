**Languages**: [[Common]], [[Goblin]]
**Ibestia** is a [[Nations|nation]] to the south of [[Laudia]], its sister nation, and borders the [[Silverhold Mountains]]. It has close relations with the [[Silverhold Dwarves]], which it uses to supply itself with metals. 

It has joined [[Laudia]] in [[The Sundering War]], and supplies it with its large amount of soldiers and weaponry.

### Geography

Ibestia is situated in the [[Empiros Peninsula]] and features a humid temperate peninsular climate. The terrain includes hills towards the [[Silverhold Mountains]] and sheer cliffs along the coast of the [[Terracine Bay]].

### Government

Ibestia is governed as a Republic, with power residing in the [[Ibestum Senate]]. Each of the senators are appointed by one of the 76 noble families. It is currently led by [[Marius Execus|Consul Marius Execus]]. 

Since the war, an organization called [[The Hand]] was taken the duties of a police force, and assisted in arresting criminals, and taking down illegal religious and arcane groups.

There is a growing group that have begun to follow [[Lucis Aurellius]] fiercely called the [[Legate Loyalists]]. The most powerful of these organization are leaders in the military, with only a couple of senators secretly having loyalties to the Legate.
