# Ibestia

## Overview

**Ibestia** is a [[Nations|nation]] to the south of [[Laudia]], its sister nation, and borders the [[Silverhold Mountains]]. It has close relations with the [[Silverhold Dwarves]], which it uses to supply itself with metals. 

It has joined [[Laudia]] in [[The Sundering War]], and supplies it with its large amount of soldiers and weaponry.

## Geography

Ibestia is situated in the [[Empiros Peninsula]] and features a humid temperate peninsular climate. The terrain includes hills towards the [[Silverhold Mountains]] and sheer cliffs along the coast of the [[Terracine Bay]].

- **Capital:** [[Imperial City]]
- **Major Cities:** [[Ravenna]], [[Luceria]], [[Ostia]], [[Ibestum]]
