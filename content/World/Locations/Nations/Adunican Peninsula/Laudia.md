**Languages**: [[Common]]

**Laudia** is a [[Nations|nation]] in eastern [[Mayl]] that is one of the nations that seated [[The Aurellian Empire]]. It lies to the north and east of the [[Silverhold Mountains]]. It borders [[Ibestia]], its sister nation, [[Alim Wastes]], and the [[Ghunal Tribes]]. Laudia owns a large piece of the southern [[Umbracine Sea]] coast, and utilizes that to increase the trade within its borders. 

On the 12th of Brymtara 713, the Prince of Laudia, [[Autia Laudia]], was found learning arcane magic from his mentor, [[Alush Bevar]]. This sparked the events of [[The Sundering War]].
