### [[Nations]] of the Sviddian Desert
##### [[Okovia]]
##### [[Piri Coast]]

The **Sviddian Desert** is in the western part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It used to be home to the [[Sviddian Mages]] before the events of [[The Magus War]]. Dry and harsh terrain fill the desert, with the only haven being the hidden nation of [[Okovia]]. Terribly strong monsters fill the desert, spawned from the cataclysmic event that was the end of [[The Magus War]].
