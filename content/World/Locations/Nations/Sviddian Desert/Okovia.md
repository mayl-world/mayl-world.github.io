**Okovia** is a [[Nations|nation]] located in the western part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It sits between the backwards flowing rivers of [[Tyg River|Tyg]] and [[Eufreit River|Eufreit Rivers]]. Although Okovia sits in the center of the [[Sviddian Desert]], the rivers somehow protect it from the wandering monstrosities and harsh weather. 

Its position in the Sviddian Desert makes severely isolated from the rest of [[Mayl]]. To most of the world, Okovia is just a rumor or myth. In fact, those who have braved the Sviddian Deserts and returned have never found Okovia.

### Government
Okovia is a democratic government, with each citizen casting a vote for laws brought up through petitions. Every person in Okovia has a voice, and is given food and shelter. 

### Economy
The rivers provide an almost magically fertile land, with the harvest always being bountiful. The foods of Okovia also have properties that prevent it from rotting, and contain healthy nutrients. 

The crops are self sustaining, and almost never dying. This means the farms are low maintenance, and require little human intervention.
