**Languages**: [[Common]], [[West Tongue]] ([[Myneth]] Based)

The **Woods of Coree** is a [[Nations|nation]] located in the [[Northern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It sits along the [[Yalen Bay]], and shares a border with the [[Golden Throne Dwarves]] to the south. The Woods are divided into counties, which are overseen by a democratically elected Count. The Woods are home to lush and peaceful forests, filled with wildlife, and bountiful trees.

### Government
The Counts of each county act as local representatives, passing local laws and addressing the needs of their county. These Counts report their needs to [[The Table of Coree]], who are compromised of renowned and trusted warriors or adventurers. The Table of Coree act as a central figure, dealing with larger intercounty disputes, and external affairs. The Table also acts as protectors of the Woods as a whole. 

The main role of government in the Woods is to encourage democratic participation, and ensure that each and every citizen is taken care of and allowed to flourish.

### Culture
The people of Coree value cooperation and unity. They usually act for the welfare of their community, and the betterment of the Woods. The communities within the Woods are close-knit and encourage support for one another. 

Because the Woods are protected by the powerful [[The Table of Coree|Table of Coree]], the people are peaceful, and have little worry over danger.
