**Languages**: [[Common]], Auric [[Dwarvish]]

The **Golden Throne Dwarves** is a [[Nations|nation]] located within the the [[Northern Continent]]. It borders [[Woods of Coree]], [[Oldraynes]], and [[The Kingdom of Sen]]. It's copious amounts of gold allows it to act as a central mint for [[Mayl]]. Almost all gold coins within circulation are minted in the [[Grand Mint Golden Dwarves]]. This brings great prosperity to these dwarves. 

### Meta Ramblings
These dwarves are very material, loving to flaunt their wealth with bodacious objects. The halls of the Golden Dwarves are lined with beautiful gold, and those that don't work within the mines, are jewelers. 

Usually the dwarven families craft intricate and large golden objects as heirlooms. 

These dwarves use their wealth to invest in various businesses around Mayl, or start trading caravans.
