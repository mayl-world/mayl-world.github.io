**Yalen Bay** sits on the eastern edge of the southern [[Icespire Peaks]] in the western part of the [[Northern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. The bay is characterized by the cold and salty, but calm waters. These waters are easy to traverse, making travel, trade, and communication easy between those that sit by the bay.
