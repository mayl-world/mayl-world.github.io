**Languages**: [[Common]], [[Norshk]] (Giant Based)

**Hjaalland** is a [[Nations|nation]] located in the northern part of the [[Northern Continent]]. It is located to the north of the [[Icespire Peaks]], and is known for its vast, and warm [[Heartwood Forest]].

### Meta Ramblings
Hjaalland is obviously the Viking nation. They are closely tied to dwarves due to their proximity to the mountains. They love raiding, and woodworking with the heartwood. Heartwood is warm to the touch and as strong as iron. 

They do a lot of fishing and some trading with southern nations. They are currently moving away from their raiding culture and is emerging as a trading power house with access to heartwood, furs, and various other good found only in the north. 

Usually, each town has a small influence, where there is a Yarl. These Yarls gather taxes, collect tribute, and organize raiding parties. Each year the Yarls convene at [[Aamlistad]], the capital, to pay tribute to the king and voice their concerns and seek guidance from the king.
