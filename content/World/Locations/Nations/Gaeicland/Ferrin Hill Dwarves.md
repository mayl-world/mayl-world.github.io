**Languages**: [[Common]], Ferric [[Dwarvish]]

The **Ferrin Hill Dwarves** are a [[Nations|nation]] located within the [[Northern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It shares a border with [[The Grand Duchy of Truap]] to the north, and [[Alim]] to the south. The hills are rough in terrain, and filled with dangerous beasts and monsters.

Due to the dangerous environment, many dwarves here are tough and respect strength. There are groups of [[Dwarf|Dwarves]] that wander the hills slaying beasts, but most work as miners or ironsmiths. Those that find battle easier than forging tend to travel and sell their sword to make a living.
