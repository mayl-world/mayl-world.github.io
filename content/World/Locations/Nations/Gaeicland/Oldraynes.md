**Languages**: [[Common]], [[Vatnese]] (Halfling Based)

**Oldraynes** is a [[Nations|nation]] located in the [[Northern Continent]]. It is bordered by the [[Golden Throne Dwarves]] in the mountains to the north, [[The Kingdom of Sen]] to the west, and [[The Grand Duchy of Truap]] to the east. Oldraynes lies on the coast of the [[Umbracine Sea]], as well as the [[Vatnid Sea]]. 

### History
The nation was founded by the pirate [[Rayn]] and his [[Rayn's Pirates]]. They were once pirates that terrorized the [[Umbracine Sea]], and eventually conquered and settled along the coast the live on today.

### Expansion
After settling along the coast of the [[Umbracine Sea]] and giving up (most) pirating, the people of Oldraynes needed a way to sustain themselves. They expanded eastward, conquering the [[Vatnid]] people, a mostly peaceful ethnic group comprised mostly of halflings.

### Culture
The people of Oldraynes are seafarers by heart, and love to display their strength. They often are boisterous, and love a good drink. They are fans of a good story of adventure, and often tell their own tales about exploits at sea. Due to their pirate heritage, they are quite greedy, and will often engage in a small bit of robbery, with the weaker party losing their valuable.
