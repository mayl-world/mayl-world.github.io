**Languages**: [[Common]], [[Sylvan]], [[Elvish]], Ferric [[Dwarvish]], [[Tryppish]] (Elvish Based)

The **Grand Duchy of Truap** is a [[Nations|nation]] located within the [[Northern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It borders [[Oldraynes]] to the west, [[Ferrin Hill Dwarves]] and [[Alim]] to the south. Within the heart of the Grand Duchy of Truap lies the [[Eruchest Forest]], where the giant [[Gaiasphon]] sits. 

The population of the Grand Duchy is very diverse, with the large forest contributing large populations of [[Orcs]], and [[Elves]]. The mixture of these races also gives rise to a sizable population of half-races. There are even a decent amount of [[Dwarf|Dwarves]] due to the proximity of the [[Ferrin Hill Dwarves|Ferrin Hills]].

### Government
At the head of the government is the [[Grand Duchess]], who holds the power and authority of ruler. She is mainly responsible for making key decision, resolving internal conflicts between factions, as well as serving as the head of diplomacy, representing the duchy on the international stage. 

The Grand Duchess delegates some of her day-to-day responsibilities to the Dukes and Duchesses she appoints. Although her authority is technically absolute across the entire nation, she allows the elves of the [[Eruchest Forest]] to govern themselves. The elves have a [[Council of Elders]] to govern the elven communities within the Grand Duchy. 

### Culture
The people of Truap enjoy music and dance of the various cultures that reside in the Duchy. There are frequent festivals that occur where cultures share their food, dance, song, and customs. 

The elves of the [[Eruchest Forest]] often have a reverence for nature, and see themselves as protectors of elven culture and the [[Eruchest Forest]]. They are usually responsible for keeping the monsters within the forest from terrorizing the rest of the realm. 

### History
Truap is a nation with a long history. They have existed from before the founding of the [[The Aurellian Empire]]. Since its founding, the Duchy had close diplomatic ties with the elves of the [[Eruchest Forest]]. 

When the [[The Aurellian Empire]] empire began to expand, the Duchy saw the threat, and quickly drafted a treaty and became a vassal of the empire.
