**Horgo** is a [[Nations|nation]] located in the [[Macchus Valley]] in the [[Southern Continent]] in [[Mayl]]. It borders [[Sholeria]] to the north, and [[The Lunar Dynasty]] to the south. However, its geographical position within the valley isolates it. It is ruled by the [[Tyrant King Olmus]] and his [[Bandit Knights]]. The [[Gestfrul River]] runs through the center of Horgo, giving fertile lands to farm.

### Government
The Tyrant King rules harshly within Horgo, paranoid about an uprising or assassination. His Bandit Knights are fiercely loyal, executing his every whim without question. Most forms of assembly, including religion is limited, with only government approved assemblies taking place. Due to this, most gatherings are done in secret.

### History
Horgo is a nation that used to be known for its honor, lush resource, and humble people. Its monarchy was a line of virtuous and wise Kings and Queens that would rule justly. Only in the last few decades has it gained the reputation of a tyrannical land.

In 672 AMW, a group of [[Goliaths]] from the [[Lunar Ridge Mountains]] descended upon the home of [[King Extus]], slaughtered his family and those loyal to him and usurped the throne, taking over the entirety of Horgo within a single year.
