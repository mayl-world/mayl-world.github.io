**The Lunar Dynasty** is a nation situated in the southern most point of [[Mayl|Mayl's]] [[Southern Continent]]. It shares a small border with [[Horgo]]. The sun never rises in the land of the Lunar Dynasty. leaving just moonlight to bathe the land. Due to the lack of sunlight, and constant moonlight, all of the plants have a red tint to them. The Lunar Dynasty is almost made up entirely of [[Moon Elves]]. 

### Government
The Lunar Dynasty is led by an group of three ascetics called the [[Star Gazers]], who watch the stars to glean wisdom and fate. They usually seclude themselves on high mountaintops, but can project their presence across [[Mayl]]. 

Beyond the guidance of the [[Star Gazers]], there is a [[Silverlight Monarch|Silverlight King or Queen]] who operates as the enlightened ruler of the Lunar Dynasty. This monarch is said to have been descended from [[Lumiara Stelthyn]], a saint said to have been created from the blood of their goddess [[Mynara]].
