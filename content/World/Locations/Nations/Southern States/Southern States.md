### [[Nations]] of the Southern States
##### [[Horgo]]
##### [[Sholeria]]
##### [[The Lunar Dynasty]]

The **Southern States** are located in the southern most part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. The states are quite isolated from one another, and the rest of Mayl, with Sholeria being the only nation that regularly interacts with its neighbors. In the north there is the [[Barbartre Woods]], which is home to some dangerous beasts, and to the south of the [[Lunar Ridge Mountains]] the sun never rises.
