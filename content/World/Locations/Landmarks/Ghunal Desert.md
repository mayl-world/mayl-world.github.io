The **Ghunal Desert** is a large desert in the norther part of the [[Southern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. It is vast with the [[Ghunal Tribes]] being the only organized group of people to live in there. The landscape is filled with sandy dunes, with the only greenery being oases scattered through the desert or along the [[Hayan River]]. The terrain in the eastern part, near the [[Silverhold Mountains]], is rocky, and tends to have canyons that dig deep into the ground.
