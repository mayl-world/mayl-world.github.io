The **Eruchest Forest** is a large and mystical forest within the heart of the [[Northern Continent]] of [[Mayl]]. Most of it is situated within [[The Grand Duchy of Truap]]. The Forest is home to the sacred [[Gaiasphon]], a massive tree with a portal to the [[Feywild]]. The Forest is home to make life, with mostly [[Elves]] making their home there. There is a substantial [[Orcs|Orc]] population within the Forest as well that regularly comes into territory conflicts with the Elves.
