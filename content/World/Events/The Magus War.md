**The Magus War** was a conflict between the [[Sviddian Mages]] and the [[Prime Material Realm|Mortal Realm of Mayl]]. It started when the Sviddian Mages, led by [[The Circle of Eight]], began collecting ancient artifacts with the intention of achieving [[Divine Powers|divine powers]]. It started as small disagreements between the Sviddian Mages and nations that held some of these artifacts, and grew into a worldwide military conflict.
