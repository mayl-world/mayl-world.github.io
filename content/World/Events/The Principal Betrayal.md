**The Principal Betrayal** is the name given to the event that where [[Autia Laudia]], the prince of [[Laudia]] was found practicing magic. This event is the direct cause of [[The Sundering War]].

## Background
[[Mayl]] experienced a calamitous war known as [[The Magus War]], where the [[Sviddian Mages]] attempted to attain divine power, decimating the worlds population in the attempt. The nations of [[Mayl]] gathered and signed [[The Unum Accord]] at the, then capital of [[The Aurellian Empire]], Castra Unum. The Accord brought an end to all forms of magic, out of fear of another cataclysmic event.

## Uncovering of Practice
On the 12th of Brymtara, 713 ASW, one of the attendants to Prince [[Autia Laudia]] overheard muttering of arcane words when passing by his room. The guards were called to investigate. They found Autia Laudia's mentor, [[Alush Bevar]], teaching Autia how to cast spells. Alush and Autia were quickly sent deep into the dungeon, and King [[Lugo Laudia]] was informed. 

One day later, Lugo descended into the dungeon to speak with his son. Upon arriving, he saw Alush treating the bruises on his son caused by the arrest. After a long and private talk between Lugo, Autia, and Alush, King Lugo decided to absolve Autia and Alush and call for the dissolving of [[The Unum Accord]].

## Aftermath
When news spread about King Lugo pardoning his son, various nations around Mayl called for action, demanding the prince and his mentor be put to death. On the 35th of Full-Light, 713 ASW, [[The Krystad Dominion]], [[The Kingdom of Sen]], [[Alim]], [[Oldraynes]], and [[The Grand Duchy of Truap]] formed a covenant to uphold [[The Unum Accord]], called [[The Unum Tribunal]]. 

Hearing about the new alliance, King Lugo agreed with Alush to send messengers across Mayl that Laudia and Ibestia would establish the [[Imperial Academy of the Arcane]]. The Imperial Academy would act as an arbiter for practitioners of the arcane, establishing rules and laws with regards to magic.
