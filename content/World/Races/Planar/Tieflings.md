**Tieflings** are a [[Races|race]] within [[Mayl]]. This is an uncommon race spawned from someone from another race becoming cursed, or making deals with unsavory beings. Depending on the circumstances of their change, their children could also be born as tieflings. Due to their origin and rarity in the world, when another encounters a tiefling they believe it to be a bad omen, and to bring bad luck. Some believe that if you venerate a tiefling you will be spared any bad luck it might give.
