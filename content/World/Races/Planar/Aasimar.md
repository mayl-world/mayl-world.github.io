**Aasimar** are a legendary [[Races|race]] within [[Mayl]]. Aasimar are humanoids who have been touched by the divine in one way or another. Usually, Aasimar are descendants of holy [[Saints]] who were blessed by [[Heron]]. Aasimar look indistinguishable from a normal member of their race, but might have a glow, with the brightness depending on the aasimars strength. Because it might be hard to tell if someone is an aasimar, no one will believe someone claiming to be one.
