**Orcs** are a [[Races|race]] within [[Mayl]]. Orcs are materialistic and territorial. They make beautiful bronze, iron, or steel vessels and armor with intricate designs. Orcs are fond enough of their belongings that those who die are often buried with everything they own. This is usually done to satisfy the desires of the dead, and to prevent potential conflicts over inheritance. 

Orcs are very territorial as well, often keeping watch over their lands to prevent trespassers. Even though they are territorial, they allow trade in and out of their lands in hopes of gathering valuable objects. 

Orcs also have a strong connection to the land they live in, and believe in a worldly spirit that lives in the land. They often perform rituals to appease it, and tend to act in a way that they believes serves that spirit. 

Orcs possess large physical strength, more than the average humanoid. They tend to make use of their strength doing physical work like moving lumber, mining, hunting, farming, and smithing.
