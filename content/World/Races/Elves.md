**Elves** are a race within [[Mayl]] that reside within their few forests, the [[Underdark]], or within the [[The Lunar Dynasty]]. Elves are inherently magical, with some being able to cast spells naturally. As a result of this Elves are written out of the arcane ban of [[The Unum Accord]].
