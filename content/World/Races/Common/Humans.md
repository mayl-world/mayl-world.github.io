**Humans** are a [[Races|race]] within [[Mayl]]. They are found within most regions of the world, and tend to live amongst other humans. Humans are the most widespread of the races, making them a common sight. It isn't uncommon for other races, such as [[Half-Elves]], [[Halflings]], and [[Gnomes]] to live within human societies. Humans are known for how diverse they are, their resilience, and rapid learning and growth.
