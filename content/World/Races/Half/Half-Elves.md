**Half-Elves** are a half-[[Races|race]] within [[Mayl]]. They are descendants of [[Humans]] and [[Elves]], and usually have a mix of traits from both races. Due to a the division of humans and elves as a result of [[The Unum Accord]], half-elves have a hard time finding place in either human or elf society. Most places that are accepting of half-elves are human settlements near elves, such as the cities within [[The Grand Duchy of Truap]].
