**King Olmus** is the name claimed by [[Threagil the Vicious]]. [[Olmus]] is a legendary figure in Horgo legend, with Threagil claiming it in the hopes that the people of Horgo will see him as a savior and a returning to greatness. 

The people of Horgo saw Threagil as he was and appended Tyrant to his claimed name to separate him from their legendary [[King Olmus]].
