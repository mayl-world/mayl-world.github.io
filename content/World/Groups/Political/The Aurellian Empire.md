**The Aurellian Empire** (Imperium Aurellium) ruled much of the coast of the [[Umbracine Sea]], and large swathes of the [[Northern Continent]]. It was founded by [[Lucius Marcus Aurellius (Emperor) |Lucius Marcus Aurellius]] in 964 SE and lasted until 72 AMW. It's downfall left a variety of states behind, with the nations of [[Laudia]] and [[Ibestia]] being a direct descendent of the Grand Empire.

### Founding
[[Lucius Marcus Aurellius (Emperor)| Lucius Aurellius]] claimed himself emperor after marching an army to [[Castra Unum]] to dissolving the [[Ludus Trade Senate]]. There was little resistance, as Aurellius was a popular figure amongst the plebian class of [[Adunia]]. 

Aurellius was crowned emperor on the 3rd of All-Saint in 964 SE. Over the next 15 years, Aurellius and his armies gained control of the entirety of the [[Umbracine Sea]] for his new empire. This control allowed trade and commerce to enrich Adunia and its people. The control of sea trade allowed Aurellius to subjugate the northern nations without fighting a single battle. 

With the northern continent under control of the Empire, Aurellius set his eyes southward. He had trouble marching his armies across the [[Ghunal Desert]], and was met with fierce resistance from its inhabitants. As a result, Aurellius commissioned his mages to create perfect warriors for his armies, [[Hobgoblins]].

During the creation of hobgoblins, Lucius Aurellius strangely disappeared; many of the plebeians believing he reached divinity. His successor, [[Marius Politia Execus]], took over as emperor and continued Aurellius' campaign southward.

They expanded to conquer much of the northern continent (Pretty much all of it except places like Hjalland and the Woods of Coree), and pushed far south enough to border the Lunar Dynasty.

- Their cultural impact left Imperial Common as the lingua franca of Mayl.
