The **Legate Loyalists** are a faction within the [[Ibestia|Ibestian]] government that is loyal to the [[Lucis Aurellius | Legate Lucius Aurellius]]. They are comprised mostly of military leaders, soldiers, and 
other figures of authority. 

### Beliefs
A core belief of these loyalists is that the Legate Aurellius is the returning of the first emperor, [[Lucius Marcus Aurellius (Emperor)|Lucius Marcus Aurellius]], believe he will return the people of the [[Adunican Peninsula]] to greatness once again. 

They also hold the idea that the current senate is an insult to the imperial heritage of Ibestia. They are standing by, ready, to help appoint a new emperor that will return them to their historical standing.

### Controversy
Due to the beliefs of the Legate Loyalists being contrary to the current government in Ibestia, many people in higher positions of power hide their loyalties.
