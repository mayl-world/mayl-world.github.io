The **Council of Three** is the governing body of the [[Silverhold Dwarves]]. They act as the legislative body, passing laws, as well as the head of the executive, commanding the army and police force within the [[Silverhold Mountains]]. The body is made up of three dwarves, each appointed from the previous holder of the position.
