The **Sviddian Mages** was a group of wizards, and sorcerers that formed to gain a greater understanding of [[Mayl]], and to be unifiers of the world. It was led by [[The Circle of Eight]], and was the cause of [[The Magus War]]. The Sviddian Mages had close ties with [[Dragons|dragons]], learning many arcane secrets from them. 

### Founding
The Sviddian Mages was founded by [[Magnus Vance]] as an effort to learn more about to deeper mechanisms of the world in order to make rational decisions. Nations from all over agreed to send the best of their mages in order to aid in this effort. The first to join Vance in his pursuit of knowledge were the powerful mages that would make up [[The Circle of Eight]]. 

### Growth
As the Sviddian Mages grew in size and power, they started to serve as a global mediator between nations, maintaining peace. This position quickly gave the Sviddian Mages a large amount of political power.

This political power eventually led to the demanding of many sacred artifacts, and eventually [[The Magus War]].
